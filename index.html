About
Dr. Shaiek Aziz Chowdhury is working as General & Laparoscopic Surgeon in Sylhet, Bangladesh and is a dedicated soul to the service of people of Sylhet. He is currently working as Associate Professor, in the Department of Surgery in Jalalabad Ragib-Rabeya Medical College, Sylhet. He passed his SSC from Chittagong Collegiate school and HSC from Chittagong College. He obtained MBBS degree in 1991 from Sylhet MAG Osmani Medical College. He entered into the world of surgery by obtaining FCPS degree in 1998. He served in different government medical colleges, among them which includes Sylhet MAG Osmani Medical college and Khulna Medical college. During his professional career, he obtained many training overseas (from Malaysia, Thailand, India etc). Besides, he is also a life member of SLSB, SOSB. Among international organizations, he is also a member of AMASI INDIA. In course of time, he performed more than 15000 laparoscopic surgeries, which includes many challenging procedures too.
